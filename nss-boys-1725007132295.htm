NSS Boys

The two units of NSS of Manohar Memorial College, Fatehabad are jointly undertaking the projects concerning social service viz. Literacy Programme, Environment, Health & Hygine, Yoga, Water Conservation etc. In addition, Group Discussion, Competitions and Debates etc. amongest the student participants are also organised from time to time. The Camps are also organised by the N.S.S. Units to clean the environments and to educate people for the important aspects of life.
